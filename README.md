# [2026] How to Check Cosmetic and Perfume Batch Codes and Production Dates

What is a cosmetic batch code? A cosmetic batch code is a combination of letters and numbers printed on the product packaging by the manufacturer to identify the product's production batch, production date, and production location. Each batch code is unique, like the product's "ID card." The main functions of a batch code include:

*   Quality traceability: Helps manufacturers track product quality issues
*   Shelf life management: Determines product freshness and validity period
*   Safety assurance: Enables quick recall of relevant batches in case of quality issues
*   Authenticity verification: Assists consumers in judging product authenticity

How to find the cosmetic batch code? Common locations: Batch codes are usually printed in the following locations:

1.  Bottom of product packaging: Most cosmetic batch codes are printed on the bottom of the box or bottle
2.  On the product label: Some products are labeled near the ingredient label
3.  Side of the bottle: Especially for perfumes and skincare products
4.  End of tube products: Such as lipsticks, foundations, etc.
5.  Side of outer packaging: Sometimes near the barcode

How to check the production date? Inquiry steps:

1.  Find the product's batch code
2.  Identify the product brand
3.  Select the corresponding brand at [https://checkcosmetic.online/](https://checkcosmetic.online/) or [https://batchcodechecker.org/](https://batchcodechecker.org/)
4.  Enter the batch code
5.  View the production date and shelf life displayed in the results

Coding rules for different brands: L'Oréal Group:

*   Format: Letter + number, e.g., "38U60"
*   The first letter represents the production year, numbers represent the production date

Estée Lauder Group:

*   Format: Letter + number, e.g., "A92"
*   Letters represent the month, numbers represent the year and date

Procter & Gamble (P&G):

*   Format: 4-digit number, e.g., "0219"
*   First two digits are the year, last two digits are the production week number

Unilever:

*   Format: Number + letter, e.g., "20C15"
*   Contains production date and production location information

Notes:

*   Different brands have different coding rules, need to select the correct brand for inquiry
*   Some niche brands may not be included in the inquiry websites
*   Fuzzy or worn batch codes may not be accurately queried
*   It is recommended to record the batch code when purchasing to avoid losing it later

Why pay attention to the production date? Product safety:

*   Avoid using expired products: Expired cosmetics may cause skin allergies or infections
*   Ensure product effectiveness: Fresh products can achieve the best results
*   Prevent bacterial growth: Expired products are prone to bacterial growth

Purchase recommendations:

*   Choose products with relatively recent production dates
*   Avoid purchasing inventory items with too old production dates
*   For skincare products, it is recommended to choose products produced within 3 months
*   Makeup products can accept products produced within 6 months
